Soil is dying...

62% of India’s soil has less than 0.5% organic content. 52% of the world’s agricultural soil ha already degraded. The United States has lost 50% of its top soil. Loss of organic content in soil turns it into sand. By 2050, 90% of Earth's soil could be degraded.

Food is no longer nutritious

60% of the world's population is deficient in nutrients like Iron due to lack of nutrients in food and soils.

We have only 45-60years

Every scientific study is clearly pointing out that we have enough soil only for another 80 to 100 crops — 45 to 60 years maximum. In another 30 years’ time, there’s going to be a serious food crisis. That is inevitable, it’s going to happen. At the same time, world population will be over 9 billion.
